FUNCTIONAL RESUME

A functional resume illustrates work experiences and abilities by skills areas in
order to highlight strengths rather than specific positions or dates of employment.

MINI RESUME

A mini-resume is a simplified version of your main resume that's easy
to share in person and hosts more information than a business card.
They allow a more unique networking experience for any occasion.
Mini-resumes often include your picture and contact information with short
lists for qualifications and experience.
